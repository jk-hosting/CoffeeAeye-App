Everything, end to end

The whole coffee journey, from bag label to golden cup.

Four connected stages — scan, match, brew, and a coffee-science lab — each tuned to the beans in your hand.

Scan

One photo. A complete bean dossier.

Snap the front and back of any bag and on-device AI reads the label for you — into a full, structured profile in seconds.

  • Origin, region, process, elevation, varietal
  • Roast level & classic→unique flavour scale
  • Roast dates, freshness rings & degas status
  • Tap to edit any field by hand
Match

Recipes that fit this bag.

A seven-signal engine scores every recipe against the scanned bean, then explains why each one fits in plain language.

  • Learns your palate from every rating
  • “Because you liked…” beans & recipes
  • Knows your real methods, ratios & origins
  • Chat live with the built-in Brew Expert
Brew

Guided, step by step.

A full-screen brew-along timer with a progress ring, per-step pours, and audio cues keeps your hands on the kettle.

  • Live weight & flow from Bluetooth scales
  • Rating advisor: concrete grind/temp/ratio moves
  • 18 grinders — settings in your own clicks
  • History, weekly stats & freshness reminders
Lab

A coffee-science laboratory.

For the obsessed: a full workflow of master-tier calculators, every result charted run over run.

  • Extraction Yield via DiFluid R2 refractometer
  • Darcy permeability, slurry temp, PSD, DTR
  • Sensory triage & gradient-descent optimiser
  • Competition routines & score sheets
